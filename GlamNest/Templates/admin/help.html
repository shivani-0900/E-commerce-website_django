How to Use the Admin Dashboard

  • Dashboard Overview: The main dashboard displays key metrics (registered buyers, pending sellers, total products, total sellers) and recent activity. Use the action cards to navigate to specific sections.
  • Managing Sellers: Access the "Pending Seller Approvals" page to review and approve/reject new sellers. Approved sellers appear in the "Our Sellers" page.
  • Managing Products: Visit the "Unapproved Products" page to review and approve/reject products. Ensure products meet quality and compliance standards before approval.
  • Viewing Users: Use the "Total Buyers" and "Total Sellers" pages to view detailed profiles of registered buyers and sellers, including contact information and other relevant details.
  • Navigation: Use the sidebar to access all sections. The sidebar can be expanded or collapsed for convenience.
  • Recent Activity: Monitor platform activities (e.g., approvals, rejections) in the "Recent Activity" section on the dashboard for quick insights.
