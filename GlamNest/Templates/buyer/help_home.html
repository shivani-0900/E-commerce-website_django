Help & FAQs

To get started on GlamNest:
  • Browse through our categories like Skincare, Haircare, and Makeup from the top navigation bar.
  • Use the search bar to find specific products or brands.
  • Click on any product to see details, reviews, and delivery info.
  • Create an account or log in to manage orders and wishlist items.

Placing an order is simple:
  1. Click "Add to Cart" on the product page.
  2. Open your cart (top right) and click "Proceed to Checkout".
  3. Enter your shipping details and choose a payment method.
  4. Click "Place Order". You will receive a confirmation email instantly.

You can track your order by:
  • Going to My Orders in your profile menu.
  • Each order will show its status (Processing, Shipped, Delivered).
  • Click "Track Order" to view courier and tracking updates.
